Our Humble Beginnings

From a small Kasi dream to Umlazi's favorite rooftop destination

2019

The Dream Begins

Legends Rooftop started as a vision to bring authentic Kasi cuisine to a modern rooftop setting. We opened our doors with just 5 tables and a simple menu focused on traditional braai and kota.

2020

Community Love

Despite challenges, the Umlazi community embraced us. We expanded our menu and started hosting live music nights, becoming a cultural hub for the township.

2022

Rooftop Expansion

We expanded our rooftop terrace to accommodate more guests and added a dedicated braai area. Our craft beer selection grew to feature local breweries.

2024

Modern Kasi Experience

Today, Legends stands as Umlazi's premier rooftop destination, blending traditional flavors with modern dining experiences while staying true to our Kasi roots.
